HEAT RETAINING FLASK

A brilliant idea, smart design and great execution have gone into making our one-of-a-kind heat retaining flask. Meeting the highest quality standards, the flask enables us to deliver hot Chai without compromising on taste and quality and retains heat for 45-60 minutes. All five layers of the pouch are approved for food industry usage with the innermost layer being certified free of harmful BPA’s. Chai Point is the first company in India to deliver hot Chai to your doorstep.

3-10-20 TO YOUR DOORSTEP

We follow a strict rule of 3-10-20. This stands for our delivery process. 3 minutes for the order to be acknowledged in our system. 10 minutes within which the order is dispatched from the time the order is placed. 20 minutes to deliver to your doorstep from our strategically placed stores.

MADE FOR YOUR CHAI

The range of available products on the Chai Point website varies from breakfast to snacks, Maggi and sweet treats. All of our food products perfectly compliment our Chai. We ensure that everything that is available to our customers is made from the best and is prepared fresh for you. Our products go through a rigorous quality check before being served in our stores.

CUSTOMER SUPPORT

We offer a platform for customer support to make sure your ordering process is smooth and hassle free. We provide quality training to our customer support executives to give you high quality assistance at all times.
